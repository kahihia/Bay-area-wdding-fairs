Terms and Conditions

Deposits & Payments A deposit is required to hold a space in any Bay Area Wedding Fairs (BAWF) trade shows. All deposits are Non-Refundable and Final. If you signed for multiple shows, deposits for all contracted shows must be received to hold space. Full payment is due 45 days prior to the show.
Cancellations and Refunds Deposits are non refundable. If cancelled within 45 days of the show, full balance is due. If a Fashion Show sponsor or Stage Services Provider cancels, the list price is due. To receive the Brides'/Grooms' List, you must be present at the entire show.
Confidentiality Agreement Booth pricing is category specific. This contract is a confidential document between BAWF and the exhibitor. A breach of the contract confidentiality agreement will result in this contract reverting to list price.
Booth Staffing & Operations The booth must be staffed throughout the duration of the show. The booth can not be shared. Displaying advertisements for business not listed on this contract is prohibited. Promoting your business outside the boundary of your assigned booth is not allowed. Intoxication during the show hours is also not permitted. Refusal to comply with these terms will result in removal from the show without refund.
Setup Booth placements are entire discretion of BAWF. Booths are to be completely set-up 30 minutes prior to the start of the show. Late arrival will result in loss of booth space without refund. No pricing displayed on banners, backdrops, and posters allowed.
Tear Down Tear down prior to the end of the show will result in $100 fee. Tear must start as soon as the show ends and must be completed within an hour.
BAWF Rights, Indemnities and Limitations All exhibitors must adhere completely by these terms and conditions and our policies and procedures at all times. Anyone not adhering to these terms will be asked to leave our shows immediately without refund. The exhibitor agrees to indemnify and hold harmless BAWF and BAWF assumes no liability, and offers no compensation of any kind for injury or loss or personal or property damage as a result of participating in this (or any) show. Exhibitors agree to use their own Business Liability Insurance for remedy in such cases. BAWF reserves all these above rights at all times.
Cancellations and Refunds All Participation in Fairs are a Binding and Final decision for the Show dates requested upon Contract signing. Deposits are NEVER refundable. Prior to 45 days advanced notice, cancellation or rescheduling is completely at the discretion of Bay Area Wedding Fairs based on several factors reviewed on a case by case basis. If an Exhibitor cancels within 45 days of any show, the full balance remains due for that show and no rescheduling is available. If a Fashion Show Sponsor or Stage Services Provider cancels within 45 days, the Contract list price is due plus any expenses required to replace their services. To receive the Brides'/ Grooms' List, you must be present at the entire show.
