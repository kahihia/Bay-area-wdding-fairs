Responsive In All Devices

Planning for your wedding day and beyond. At our shows, you will find everything you need from starting the planning process with budgeting, finding a wedding venue and bridal gown to booking wedding photographer, florist, caterer, dj and more.

#} {#
#} {#
#}

Friends

Invite friends and family that are part of your planning group. Invite via email or import from your Facebook, Gmail or Yahoo mail account. Keep all your communication private within your invited friends.

My Planner

No need to maintain Google Doc for your event plans. Create your planning tasks or use YapJoy’s pre-created commonly used planning tasks. Assign a budget to the tasks and publish to the related vendors. Receive bids on those plans and award bids to the ones that meet your needs and budget.

Yapper

An open forum where you can find helpful information on various topics. Search on different categories or keywords. Its a place where you meet Yappers and you get a chance to Yap all you want.

Wedding Professionals

Connect and follow professionals that YapJoy recommends based on what you are looking for. Check out their profile, rating and reviews. Invite them to your planning tasks and receive bids from them.

Events & Tasks

Create events and tasks. Assign to your friends and family. Send reminders and check them off when completed.

Seating Charts

Create seating chart for your event. Assign seats to the users and email them where they will be seated (feature coming soon..)
