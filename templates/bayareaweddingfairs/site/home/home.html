PLANNING

Bay area wedding fairs is your portal to planning for your wedding day and beyond. At our shows, you will find everything you need from starting the planning process with budgeting, finding a wedding venue and bridal gown to booking wedding photographer, florist, caterer, dj and more.

SHOPPING

Our fairs are one stop shop for all your planning needs from beginning to end. From booking your venue to finalizing details before your wedding with wedding decor, gifts favors and even help with honeymoon travel. Our preferred partner vendors are the cream of the crop! They exhibit at most of our shows and offer show specials. Make sure you attend their booths at our fairs and enter to win their season's grand prizes.
